RECRUITMENT - IT’S TIME FOR CHANGE

Recruiters are in the top 10 least trusted professions, and for good reason. Far too many recruitment agencies over-promise and under-deliver, making the search process ineffective, expensive and frustrating. This, plus the poor sales techniques used in the sector has created distrust between recruiters and employers.

A recruiter himself, our founder Andrew says "I've grown to be ashamed of the recruitment industry. Traditional business models are unjust and archaic, and it's time for a proper shake-up. It's time for a platform that connects recruiters directly to Employers, cutting out the agency. Cut out the greedy agency bosses and you reduce your costs by at least 50%, straight off the bat. Offer the recruiter the whole fee and they will climb mountains for you."
